# Featzy

Featzy is a group project realized as a end of year Student project.

## The project and it's concept

Featzy is a solution that we created to answer a few questions :

- Have you ever felt like your dish took forever to be ordered and brought to your table ?
- Have you ever thought that you didn't have enough time to either savour your meal and talk to your friend, coworkers or co-eaters ?
- What about paying the bill or ordering for a group ? It always takes too much deliberations and efforts to decides when a simple solution can be made to lessen all thoses inconvenience.

That _solution_ is **Featzy** ! A web app that leat you not only reserve a restaurant in advance but also order your own dish and let each of your guest order and pay for their own command.
No more wait at the restaurant, no more debate about paying, no more loss of time grouping all order for one person to take charge.

**Featzy ? Eating out made Easy**
